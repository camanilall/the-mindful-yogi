The Mindful Yogi is a hub for beginners to learn how to connect the mind and body using meditation and yoga techniques.

What is Meditation and Yoga?

What is Yoga?

    Yoga which means to "yoke" or to bind is the practice which enables us to connect the mind and the body together through movement.
  • Traditionally there are 8 limbs of Yoga that detail morals to live by and as they ascend focus on the practice of meditation.
  • There are many different types of yoga from more intense Hatha and Vinyasa practice or slower Yin Yoga.
  • Don't be afraid to try and few different types and decide which type works best for you!
  • Yoga is an inclusive practice and should be taught in this way.

What is Meditation?

    Meditation the practice of focusing the mind on something and aiming to not lose attention of the focus. Usually we practice meditation by focusing on the breath.
  • Meditation is practice that requires patience so don't be disheartened if you don't see progress straight away.
  • Long-term meditation will help to improve focus and reduce distractions known as 'monkey-mind'.
  • Meditation is also known to be a great stress-reliever.
  • Practising meditation will help you to connect better with your body.
